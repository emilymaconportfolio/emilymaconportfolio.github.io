Bio

I am a visual designer, illustrator, letterer, artist, colorist, and bezier wrangler — based out of San Francisco. 

While I'm proud of my melting pot of skillsets and interests, my most valuable characteristic is the ability to work fast without sacrificing quality, attention to detail, or losing sight of the broader goal.

I love painting, traveling, making jewelry, and spotting typefaces in the wild.
